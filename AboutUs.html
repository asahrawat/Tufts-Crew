About Tufts Crew

The Shoemaker Boathouse

The William A. Shoemaker Boathouse donated by David Shoemaker, was erected in 2006 and held its first regatta in 2007. Our modern boathouse incoroporates classic features of boathouses around the country as well as emerging technologies in the sport of crew, such as our indoor boat simulators and our ergometers with VR headset attachments.

Shoemaker Boathouse Location

  • 0.4 miles from Wellington Orange Line with shuttle service to and from Rivers Edge
  • 4.0 miles from Boston
  • 3.5 miles from Cambridge
